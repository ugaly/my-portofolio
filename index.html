About me

I’m a passionate and results-driven Full Stack Developer with hands-on experience building and managing high-performance applications across web and mobile platforms. I specialize in designing scalable backend systems, crafting responsive frontends, and deploying secure, production-ready solutions.

With a strong foundation in backend frameworks like Spring Boot, Laravel, and Django, I’ve engineered robust APIs and business logic for applications across various industries. On the frontend, I work seamlessly with Next.js, React, and Angular, delivering intuitive and performant user experiences.

I’m well-versed in databases such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB, and I adapt my choice based on project needs for optimal data handling and scalability.

On the mobile side, I develop cross-platform apps using React Native, and also create native Android applications with both Kotlin and Java.

My development workflow is powered by modern version control systems like GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket, ensuring clean collaboration and continuous integration.

As part of my DevOps and infrastructure experience, I regularly use Docker to containerize applications, ensuring consistency across development, testing, and production environments. I also manage deployments on various Linux servers, focusing on security, server hardening, and performance tuning.

I also have hands-on experience in data analytics and dashboarding, where I transform raw data into meaningful insights using tools like Pandas, Matplotlib, and Plotly on the backend, and Chart.js, Recharts, and React-based dashboards on the frontend. I design and develop custom reporting dashboards that help businesses track KPIs, monitor operations, and make informed decisions in real time.

Beyond the technical stack, I value clean code, performance optimization, and problem-solving. Whether working independently or as part of a team, I strive to deliver solutions that are reliable, maintainable, and aligned with business goals.
